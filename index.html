Ingredients

Gochu Flavour

  • Fermented Soybeans (Meju)

    The foundation of traditional Korean fermentation, delivering deep, rich umami.

  • Artisanal Gochujang

    Korean fermented red chili paste that adds warmth, color, and balanced heat.

  • Korean Garlic

    Bold and aromatic, a cornerstone of Korean cuisine.

  • Fresh Ginger

    Provides brightness and a subtle spicy freshness to balance the fermentation.

  • Rice Syrup (Ssal-yeot)

    A traditional Korean sweetener offering natural sweetness and depth.

  • Toasted Korean Sesame Oil

    Intensely fragrant, adding a nutty and luxurious finish.

Sweet Chili
& Lime

  • Bird’s Eye Chili

    Small, fiery Thai chilies that deliver the authentic, vibrant heat found in Thai cuisine.

  • Kaffir Lime Juice

    Traditional citrus with an intense, aromatic flavor, here lightly smoked for depth.

  • Nam Pla (Fish Sauce)

    Essential Thai umami, providing depth and savory richness.

  • Lemongrass

    Fresh stalks bruised or minced to release fragrant, citrusy aroma.

  • Coconut Sugar

    Gently sweet, caramel-like sugar typical in Thai cooking, balancing heat and citrus.

  • Galangal

    A zesty, aromatic root that adds bright, citrusy warmth typical of Thai cooking.

White Miso
Umami Sauce

  • White Miso

    The heart of Japanese cuisine, providing a smooth, rich umami base.

  • Soy Sauce

    Adds depth, savoriness, and perfectly complements the miso flavor.

  • Mirin

    A traditional Japanese sweet rice wine that delivers gentle sweetness and balance.

  • Dashi Powder

    A touch of classic Japanese umami from kombu and bonito, enhancing complexity.

  • Rice Vinegar

    Brings a subtle acidity to brighten flavors and harmonize the sauce.

  • Fresh Ginger

    Adds freshness and a light, aromatic spice to enliven the umami.
